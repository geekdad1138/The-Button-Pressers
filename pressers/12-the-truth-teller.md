# The Truth Teller

## **Timnit Gebru (2018/2020)**

### **The Human**

Timnit is a brilliant researcher at the intersection of computer vision and ethics. While the rest of the valley is celebrating how "smart" AI has become, Timnit is looking at the cracks. She sees that these "miracle" systems are failing to recognize faces that look like hers and are absorbing the worst biases of the internet.

### **The Button**

She "presses the button" on **Accountability**. She co-authors "Gender Shades" and later "On the Dangers of Stochastic Parrots." She chooses to speak truth to power within the very institutions (Google/Microsoft) that are building these models, warning that "Bigger" isn't always "Better" if it comes at the cost of safety and truth.

### **The Ripple**

Her decision to publish—even at the cost of her job—changed the industry forever.

* **The Ethics Era:** She forced every major AI lab to create "Ethics and Society" teams.
* **Critical Thinking:** She moved the conversation from "What can AI do?" to "Should AI do this?" She pressed the button on **Responsible AI**, ensuring that the "shoulders" we build next are more stable and more human.

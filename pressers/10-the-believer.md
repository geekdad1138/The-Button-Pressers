# The Believer

### **Ilya Sutskever: The Bet on Scale (2015/2023)**

#### **The Human**

Ilya is a student of Geoffrey Hinton (our "Stubborn" presser). He is a man of deep, almost mystical intensity regarding AI. In the mid-2010s, most people thought AI was just a neat trick for identifying cats in photos. Ilya, however, believed that if you just gave the math enough data and enough compute, it would "start to feel" things about the world.

#### **The Button**

Ilya "pressed the button" on **Scaling Laws**. As a co-founder of OpenAI, he pushed for the creation of GPT-2 and GPT-3, betting billions of dollars and years of research on the idea that "More is Different." While others were trying to write clever new algorithms, Ilya was the one saying, "Feed it more of the internet. Bigger. More GPUs."

#### **The Ripple**

This choice shifted the entire trajectory of the 2020s.

* **The LLM Era:** We didn't get ChatGPT because of a brand-new math discovery in 2022; we got it because Ilya insisted on scaling the 2017 Transformer architecture to an unbelievable size.
* **The AGI Debate:** He pressed the button on the conversation we are having today: *Can a machine actually think?* He moved AI from "tool" to "entity" in the public consciousness.

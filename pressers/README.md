# The Gallery of Pressers

> "Most of the big things in the world are done by people who are tired, annoyed, or just trying to get a game to work."

This folder is a collection of **human moments**. While the rest of the world looks at the massive architectures of AI and computing, we look at the individual in the chair at 2:00 AM. 

## The Rules of the Story
Unlike our other technical documentation, these entries follow a different set of constraints:

1. **No Academic Jargon:** We don't care about the "parallelization of tensors" here; we care about the researcher who was too stubborn to quit.
2. **The Micro-Moment:** We look for the specific decision—the "Button Press"—that triggered the avalanche.
3. **The Human Why:** We focus on the emotion: boredom, necessity, ego, or curiosity.

## How to add a Presser
We are looking for stories of **Accidental Greatness**. If you know of a moment where a small human choice led to a massive technological ripple, create a new file using the template:

- **The Human:** The context of their life/mood at the time.
- **The Button:** The specific choice or action.
- **The Ripple:** How the world changed because of it.

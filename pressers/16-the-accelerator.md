# The Accelerator

## **Sam Altman (2022)**

### **The Human**

It’s late 2022. OpenAI has incredible technology (GPT-3.5), but it’s mostly tucked away in an API used by developers. The world knows AI is "coming," but it still feels like a lab experiment. Sam Altman, a veteran of Silicon Valley’s startup machine, realizes that the tech is ready for the world—but is the world ready for the tech?

### **The Button**

On November 30, 2022, Sam "presses the button" on **Accessibility**. He decides to wrap their most powerful model in a simple, free, chat interface and release it to the public as **ChatGPT**. It wasn't a "new" scientific discovery that day; it was a decision to stop being a research lab and start being a product company.

### **The Ripple**

ChatGPT became the fastest-growing consumer application in history.

* **The AI Arms Race:** It forced Google, Microsoft, and Meta into an overnight sprint, changing the trajectory of every tech company on earth.
* **The Public Consciousness:** He pressed the button on the "AI Era." Before that day, AI was something in movies; after that day, it was something people used to write emails and do homework. He accelerated the future by 10 years in a single afternoon.
